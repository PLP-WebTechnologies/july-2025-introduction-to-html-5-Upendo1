About Us

Green Valley School provides a balanced curriculum focused on academic excellence, character development, and community engagement. Our inclusive environment supports learners of all backgrounds and abilities.

Our Programs

Early Childhood Education

Our preschool program lays the foundation for lifelong learning through play-based, student-centered education.

Primary School

Students in grades 1–6 enjoy a structured and stimulating curriculum aligned with national education standards.

Secondary School

Grades 7–12 prepare students for higher education and careers, with advanced science, technology, and humanities tracks.

Admissions

Enrollment is open year-round. We welcome applications from all families who value academic growth and holistic development.
